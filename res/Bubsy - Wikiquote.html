Bubsy

From Wikiquote
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Bubsy was a cartoon bobcat character in a platform game much like the 2-D games of the early nineties similar to Mario and Sonic. Bubsy wears his white trademark Exclamation! shirt but no pants or shoes. The enemies and objectives of Bubsy Bobcat tended to vary from game to game, but Bubsy's comedic "purrsonality" remained basically the same.
